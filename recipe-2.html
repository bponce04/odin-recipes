Chilaquiles

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of frying oil
  • 30 (6-inch) tortillas
    • Make sure they're torned into strips.
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onion
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 7 ounces of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of shredded Mazorella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Gather ingredients:
    • Once gathered, heat the oil in a large skillet
    • Make sure the skillet is set to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Fry tortillas and onions:
    • Fry until crisp and golden brown (not regular brown!)
  3. Remove to a paper towel to drain:
    • Ensure you drain the skillet, but leave a thin residue of oil
  4. Place skillet over medium heat:
    • Return tortilla strips and onions back into skillet
    • Season tortilla strips with salt
  5. Stir in tomato sauce and water:
    • Reduce heat and simmer until strips thicken
    • Do this for about 10 minutes
  6. Sprinkle with cheese:
    • Sprinkle to your liking
    • Keep stirring until cheese melts
